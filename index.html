How to Play

Press the play tab in the nav bar to start playing

When first starting to play the transport memory game take your time the score which will display after all cards have been matched is not taking into account time you spent so make sure to take note of where the images are as the less moves you take the higher score you can get as moves are taken into account when playing.

The playing cards give visual feedback when a card is flipped it it wll get a blue outline around the edge to show which image you have flipped so you dont forget which one you picked. When you select two cards the game will check if they match and if they do then you will get a green outline and if they are not a match you will get a red outline until they are reset.

If you expierence any issues with the game or notice any bugs please feel free to contact me through the contact tab
